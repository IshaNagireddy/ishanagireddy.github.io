Experiences

What I've spent my time doing!

Over the past few summers and school years, I'm greatful to have had the opportunity to work on numerous cool projects in multiple organizations. Keep reading to learn more about what I've contributed to!

RTX/BBN Software Engineer Intern

As a software engineer intern at RTX, I worked on the project ASTARTE, a DARPA funded project preventing friendly-fire incidents with 98% accuracy. I helped develop. Hasty Attack Course of Action (HA COA), which cuts planning time by 50%. I also seperated the HA-COA generator from the codebase allowing clients to solely use HA functionality. Finally, I also developed the foundation for more streamlined versions for multiple data classes in ASTARTE.

Harvard Medical School, Research Intern

I used Transformers in RNA sequencing and function loss of OPTN. I developed OptiCare: An app containing 2 CNN models for image detection of cataracts/glaucoma. Through OptiCare, I also developed a 3D tool linking sound reflection to intraocular pressure for glaucoma tracking. Overall, OptiCare brings glaucoma and cataracts screening from $500 to $5. A 99% cost decrease.

MIT, Mantis Rising Scholar

As a Mantis Rising Scholar, I helped develop the online ata visualization platform Mantis, and was part of the AI agents team. I worked with the OpenAI API to prompt engineer agents with different expertise to assist users. I also got the opportunity to listen in on multiple ML/tech conversations with Dr. Manolis Kellis and other MIT professors.

IOMICS Corporation, Machine Learning Intern

I modified Kolgorov Arnold Networks (KANs) to create new network-like visualization techniques. I worked directly on an external server and ran large genomics data mining tasks with FP-Growth. I also got the opportunity to work directly and report back to the CTO, Joseph Gormley, of IOMICS Corporation.

UMass Chan Medical School, Research Intern

As a research intern, I developed TalkToMe: an app containing 2 CNN models for image detection of cataracts and glacuoma. I also got the opportunity to present TalkToMe to Jim Walton (heir to the fortune of Walmart) and bard members. I also lead development on a second application called ClinicApp. ClinicApp is a mobile app allowing doctors to conduct studies and run custom imported ML models.
